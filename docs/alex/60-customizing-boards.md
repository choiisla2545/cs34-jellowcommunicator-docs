## Customizing Boards

- Access settings to create personal boards and organize your favorite icons.

In creating a customized board, tap the three lines on top right. Then you'll see My Boards there.

Tap the add board then you'll be able to see different things on the side. Once you done it, you have to name your board, for different scenarios like daily activities or going out!

Add a photo related to it. You can apply different language and accent too. Then press continue!
