## Customizing Boards

- Access settings to create personal boards and organize your favorite icons.

In creating a customized board, tap the three lines on top right. Then you'll see **My Board** there.

Tap the **plus icon** to add a board then you'll be able to see different things on the side. Once you done it, you have to name your board! It can be for different scenarios like daily activities or going out!

Add a photo related to it. You can apply different language and accent too. Then press **continue**!

When you have created a customized board, it's time to put your choice of words in the board.

### Adding Icons

When adding icons and words to your custom board, you can go through the different categories for choose the icons that you want.

When you have selected the icons, make sure to press **next** to apply those icons. After that it will show you on how it would look like. 

Tap **save** to apply the changes for your board. When it has been saves, you now have your customized board with the icons and words you have chosen.

### Adding Changes in the Board

When you want to change things in your board.

Go back to the **My Board** to and tap the **pencil icon** to do some changes. Tap the board you want to edit and now you can go back adding icons or removing icons from the board. 

You can **reset** to remove all icons or you can go to a category to **select all** icons from a category to add all of it in the board. Once done doing changes, you can tap **next** to get the preview and finally tap **save** to apply changes.
