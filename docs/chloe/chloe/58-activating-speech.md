## Activating Speech
1. Open the **Jellow** app.
2. Help your child choose a category by tapping one o the **big picture/icon** buttons.
3. Ask your child to tap the **picture/icon** or word they want to say.
4. Once the picture is selected, tap the **Speak Button** (or the **speech/play button**, depending on your device).
5. The app will read the message out loud.
