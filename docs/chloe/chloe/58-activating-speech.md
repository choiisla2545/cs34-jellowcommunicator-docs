## Activating Speech
1. Open the Jellow app.
2. Help your child hoose a category by tapping one o the big picture buttons.
3. Ask your child to tap the picture or word they want to say.
4. Once the picture is selected, tap the Speak Button (or the speech/play button, depending on your device).
5. The app will read the message out loud.

Tip for Parents and Caregivers:
Encourage your child to listen to the spoken message and repeat the process as often as needed. Giving praises for every attempt can help build confidence and make communication more enjoyable.
