## Activating Speech Playback
Jellow allows users to communicate by selecting pictures or typing their own words. The app can then read the selected or typed message aloud using speech playback.

1. Open the **Jellow** app.
2. Help your child choose a category by tapping one of the **big picture/icon** buttons.
<img width="512" height="237" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/50f4f24c-b136-45b0-8d55-765f9c74689d" />
 

3. If the desired word or phrase is not available, tap the **Keyboard icon** and type the word, or message you want to communicate.
<img width="512" height="237" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/09a1d9ea-dcf6-43be-9198-b72beaf758dc" />


4. Once a picture is selected or a message is typed, tap the **Speak button**.
<img width="512" height="237" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6ad18fef-e62f-4282-bf45-b52a0bd52e57" />


5. The app will read the message out loud.
