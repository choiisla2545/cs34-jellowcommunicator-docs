## Choosing a Voice Setting
Choose a voice that is clear and easy for your child and the people around them to understand. A comfortable speaking speed can also make communication easier during daily activities.

1. Open **Jellow** app.
2. Tap the **Settings** button. 
3. Look for the **Voice or Speech Settings** option and tap it.
4. Adjust the speech speed or volume to a level that is comfortable for your child. <img width="512" height="237" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/39798da8-a32d-4940-9079-0eb0e68011df" />

5. Test the new voice by electing the **Speech Demo button**. <img width="512" height="237" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e27cd9b2-d62b-4375-8aa8-61086a13f783" />

6. Tap **Save** and simply return to the main screen.
