## Choosing a Voice Setting
Choose a voice that is clear and easy for your child and the people around them to understand. A comfortable speaking speed can also make communication easier during daily activities.

1. Open **Jellow** app.
2. Tap the **Settings** button. 
3. Look for the **Voice or Speech Settings** option and tap it.
4. Choose the voice you would like your child to use.
5. If available, adjust the speech speed or volume to a level that is comfortable for your child. <img width="2048" height="946" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/39798da8-a32d-4940-9079-0eb0e68011df" />

6. Tap **Save** or simply return to the main screen if the changes are saved automatically.
7. Test the nwe voice by electing a **picture/icon** and using the **Speak button**.
