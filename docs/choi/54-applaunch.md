## Launching the App

To properly launch the Jellow Communicator interface:

### Step 1: Locate the App
- Find the *Jellow Communicator* icon on your device home screen or app drawer

---

### Step 2: Open the Application
- Tap the app icon once
- Wait for the loading screen to appear

---

### Step 3: Wait for Initialization
- Allow the app a few seconds to load all communication boards
- Do not tap repeatedly while loading

---

### Step 4: Confirm Successful Launch
The app is successfully launched when:
- The *home communication board* is visible
- Category buttons are displayed on screen
- Icons are ready for selection

---

### Optional Check
- Ensure device volume is on for speech output
- Check that accessibility permissions are enabled if required

---
